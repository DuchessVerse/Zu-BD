It pulls us in every direction; school, work, becoming, unlearning, relearning, chasing money, searching for purpose, holding family together, trying to belong while still trying to understand who we even are. You are not just growing older, Zurah. You are becoming. And I am so, so proud of the woman you are unfolding into. Forever yours, in laughter, in silence, in prayer, in everything 🤍
